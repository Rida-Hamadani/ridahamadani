The War Veteran

Little girl in meadows
Hides and seeks

She rolls under willows
In the autumn leaves

Her smile has windows
And he peeks

His face of furrows
Finds some peace

Her melody echoes
Then drums pierce

Her fireworks arrows
He reprieves

Her, or so he wished
As windows form a door

Whose hinges squeak
Like leather boots
Or the toy, on the floor

And kindly, oh so softly
He tickles the sockets,
Of her eyes so that

She can hear how
The city screams
